## Highlights
- 5 frameworks from top consulting companies that help in Product and Business:
  1/ Ansoff Matrix 
  ![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/F89xWVpWgAAGqNE.png) ([View Tweet](https://twitter.com/bandanjot/status/1715714615886094754))
- Ansoff Matrix allows you to think on the lines of products and markets , leading to 4 possible outcomes:
  Selling new products to existing market
  Selling existing products to new markets
  Selling new products to new markets 
  Selling new products to new markets ([View Tweet](https://twitter.com/bandanjot/status/1715714617408651615))
- 2/ McKinsey 9 box matrix
  helps you know which business units you should invest into/ maintain or divest: 
  ![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/F89xW07X0AIPLkC.png) ([View Tweet](https://twitter.com/bandanjot/status/1715714624136319105))
- 3/ Profit - Root Cause Analysis
  Gives you all possible scenarios to dissect profit and finally arrive at conclusion what levers on revenue side and cost side can be improved 
  ![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/F89xXPRWoAEh9i1.png) ([View Tweet](https://twitter.com/bandanjot/status/1715714630524297714))
- 4/ Action Prioritization Matrix
  Similar to effort vs impact framework, it allows you to figure out how to categorize your ideas and pursue them in what sequence. 
  ![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/F89xXldXsAAXtKd.png) ([View Tweet](https://twitter.com/bandanjot/status/1715714638917099791))
- 5/ DARCI 
  For more complex projects or initiatives, it helps to allocate roles early. I have also used the framework as a starting point for documents 
  ![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/F89xYJKWwAAvZjs.png) ([View Tweet](https://twitter.com/bandanjot/status/1715714646169031151))
