## Highlights
- ChatGPT will kill businesses that don't change.
  The 6 businesses disrupted (& how you adjust to make a fortune): ([View Tweet](https://twitter.com/BrianFOConnor/status/1603032772804857856))
- 1/ Personal Services
  Ex: Interior design, travel planners, architects
  Disruption:
  AI produces custom plans and designs (ex: https://t.co/b8Q0K9KUw4)
  Winning firms will spend more time understanding customer needs and use AI to reduce time creating designs. 
  ![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/Fj8d1WPXwAInl2q.jpg) ([View Tweet](https://twitter.com/BrianFOConnor/status/1603032799535144961))
- 2/ Consulting Firms
  Disruption:
  AI answers your business questions impersonating business experts
  Ex: ask Elon how to grow your online business: https://t.co/sbWHE8rfwV
  How to win:
  Include AI experts on your team.
  Apply that expertise to a customer's specific situation. 
  ![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/Fj8d2F9WAAcJw3_.jpg) ([View Tweet](https://twitter.com/BrianFOConnor/status/1603032811564318721))
- 3/ Content Creation
  Disruption:
  AI creates general listicles, blogs, and newsletters.
  How to win:
  Use ChatGPT to:
  • Brainstorm topics
  • Research ideas faster
  Differentiate by:
  • Deeper insights
  • Find unique applications in content
  • Evoke emotion with storytelling 
  ![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/Fj8d3D6WIAAodt7.jpg) ([View Tweet](https://twitter.com/BrianFOConnor/status/1603032830845587461))
- 4/ Developers
  Disruption:
  AI writes and debugs code.
  How developers need to use AI to win:
  • Make software faster
  • Debug code quickly
  • Write in languages they didn't know
  Winners will use AI to create faster product iterations to better resolve customer pains. 
  ![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/Fj8d4PlXgAAairL.jpg) ([View Tweet](https://twitter.com/BrianFOConnor/status/1603032850156208129))
- 5/ Graphic Design
  Disruption:
  AI makes you designs.
  How to win:
  Use AI for faster turnarounds with clients so you can have more iterations and get them custom designs
  h/t: @Pejkster for AI-created images (using MidJourney) 
  ![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/Fj8d6Q0XwAQ0s7_.jpg) ([View Tweet](https://twitter.com/BrianFOConnor/status/1603032885455454208))
- @pejkster 6/ Educators & course creators
  Disruption:
  Students can use ChatGPT to quickly learn and get answers to specific questions
  How to win:
  Hard skills are now less relevant.
  Focus content on networking and applying concepts. 
  ![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/Fj8d7Z9XkAM1kn0.jpg) ([View Tweet](https://twitter.com/BrianFOConnor/status/1603032904132694019))
